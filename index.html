Welcome to Chonky's Privacy Policy! We keep things simple and transparent here.

What We Collect

Actually, almost nothing! We only store:

  • Your animation preferences
  • Reminder settings (if you use them)

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Login credentials
  • Messages or communications
  • Any other user data

Permissions We Use

Host Permissions: Required to display Chonky on supported platforms (X/Twitter, Discord, Telegram)

Storage: Saves your preferences locally

Active Tab: Allows Chonky to appear and animate on your current webpage

Data Storage

All preferences are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

Changes to Policy

If we update this policy, we'll notify you through the extension.
